INDIVIDUAL TAXPAYER REGISTRATION 

FORM

This form is meant for all Individual taxpayers except those who operate as sole traders. The individuals required to fill this form include employers, directors and partners. In the case of sole traders, they should use the Business Taxpayer Registration Form.

BUSINESS TAXPAYER REGISTRATION

FORM

This form is applicable to businesses and sole traders. It should be completed by the designated officer or the business owner. For individuals who are not sole traders, they should use the Individual Taxpayer Registration Form.
